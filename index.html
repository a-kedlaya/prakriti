The Three Energies

Understanding the Doshas

Every person is a unique blend of three fundamental bio-energies called Doshas.

💨
Air + Space

Vata

The Energy of Movement

Light Dry Cold Fast Mobile

🧠 Controls

  • Movement — breathing, blood flow, nerve signals
  • Thinking and creativity

🧍 Dominant Traits

  • Thin body, fast talkers
  • Energetic but tire quickly
  • Creative and imaginative

⚠️ When Imbalanced

  • 😟 Anxiety & restlessness
  • Dry skin, constipation
  • Insomnia
Most Common
🔥
Fire + Water

Pitta

The Energy of Transformation

Hot Sharp Intense Oily

🧠 Controls

  • Digestion and metabolism
  • Body temperature
  • Intelligence and focus

🧍 Dominant Traits

  • Medium build, strong digestion
  • Smart, focused, ambitious
  • Natural leaders

⚠️ When Imbalanced

  • 😡 Anger & irritability
  • Acidity, ulcers
  • Excess heat, sweating
🌍
Earth + Water

Kapha

The Energy of Structure

Heavy Slow Stable Cool Smooth

🧠 Controls

  • Body structure — bones, muscles
  • Immunity and strength
  • Emotions like calmness and love

🧍 Dominant Traits

  • Strong, heavier body
  • Calm, patient, loyal
  • Good stamina

⚠️ When Imbalanced

  • 😴 Laziness & lethargy
  • Weight gain
  • Congestion, cold
⚡

Many people are dual-dosha — a blend of two energies like Vata-Kapha or Pitta-Kapha. Our AI model recognizes all combinations.
